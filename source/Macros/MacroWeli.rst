========
``weli``
========

Peaceman type well impedance.

* Group 1 - ITYPEWELL, KEYWEL1, PARMWEL1, PARMWELL2, PARMWEL3

Enter one line per model defined, terminated with a blank line.

* Group 2 - JA, JB, JC, IZONEWELL
