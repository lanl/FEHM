========
``frlp``
========

Relative permeability factors for residual air effect.

Group 1 - 	JA, JB, JC, RLP_FAC (liquid), RLP_FAC (vapor) (JA, JB, JC - defined on `JA, JB, JC, PROP1, PROP2, . . . <Macro20058.html>`_)

+------------------+--------+------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Input Variable   | Format | Description                                                      |
+==================+========+==================================================================+
| RLP_FAC (liquid) | real   | Residual liquid relative permeability value (i = 1 to n0).       |
+------------------+--------+------------------------------------------------------------------+
| RLP_FAC (vapor)  | real   | Residual vapor relative permeability value (i = n0 + 1 to 2*n0). |
+------------------+--------+------------------------------------------------------------------+
